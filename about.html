Kalna or Ambika Kalna is a town in Burdwan district situated beside the western banks of river Bhagirathi of West Bengal. This place is devoted to Goddess Kali, also known as Maa Ambika. Hindus and Bengalis, consider it as a religious place and a home to several famous historical sites. We went to Samudragarh-Dhatrigram cluster which is 15 kms away from Kalna, situated on the agriculturally rich alluvial plains between the Bhagirathi, Ajay, and Damodar rivers

Kalna has a tropical rainy climate. This area has good amount of rain and temperature ranges from 17 to 18 °C in the winter to 30 to 32 °C in the summer. In Kalna, the wind blows with an average speed of 12.9 mph in July, which makes it the windiest month
