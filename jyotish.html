JYOTISH DEBNATH

Jyotish Debnath belongs to a heritage family of Jamdani weavers who have been involved in the practice of Jamdani craft since 1930s when they shifted from Bangladesh to India. Their inputs to the development of Jamdani has been immense, they holistically manufacture fabrics with the inception of 600 and 700 count muslin, the only two ever produced in the world, weighing just 10 grams. His son, Rajib Debnath is also involved in the family business and handles the technology and marketing sector of the business. He used to work in the IT sector but soon realised that he wanted to contribute to his own family tradition.
