KRISHNA BASAK

Krishna Basak from Samudragarh District has been working with the National Institute of Fashion Technology for design development since many years. He is very fond of Jamdani textiles and enjoys working and developing a modernized version of the craft. Krishna Da works on more contemporary designs with a minimal and modernist colour palette. He makes various designs on Jacquard and he experiments with different designs. He puts in the effort of learning new techniques through online resources and then implements in his personal explorations. Krishan Da says that these people are focused on sustaining their business and that they do not really care about carrying forward the treasured tradition of Jamdani weaving. His family has been practicing weaving for 4 generations. His father worked on original resham sarees which were made of pure raw materials which are almost extinct now.
